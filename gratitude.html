Hackwell’26 – Event Highlights & Gratitude

Hackwell’26 was successfully conducted with immense enthusiasm, innovation, and collaboration. The event served as a dynamic platform for aspiring minds to showcase their creativity and problem-solving skills in the domain of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science.

We express our heartfelt gratitude to our respected Principal, Dr. D. Valavan, and Secretary, Shri S. Ravindran, along with our esteemed institution, for their continuous support and encouragement in making this event a grand success.

We extend our sincere thanks to Dr. S. Ravimaran (CoE, HoD – AI & DS), and Mr. Sadheesh, Faculty coordinator, for their exceptional guidance, planning, and efforts in organizing Hackwell’26 seamlessly.

We are deeply grateful to our sponsors – Microsoft, IEI, and Tech Shiksha – for their valuable support and contribution towards the success of this event.

A special note of appreciation to our distinguished jury members:

  • Mr. Sridharan V, Associate Vice President at Hitachi Group
  • Mr. Vinoth Kumar N, Data Scientist at PwC
  • Mr. Rohith Kumar R, Founder of Euredas.ai

We thank them for their excellent mentorship, insightful evaluation, and for being incredibly kind and approachable to all participants. We also extend our gratitude to the jury members of the preliminary round of Hackwell’26 for their valuable time and effort.

We are honored by the presence of our Chief Guest, Mr. Ramesh Kumar P K, Additional General Manager at BHEL Trichy, and we sincerely thank him for sharing valuable insights into today’s technological landscape and for distributing the prizes.

Our heartfelt appreciation goes to our Chief Student Coordinators, Mr. Rishi Kumar S and Mr. Dhivakar B (Final Year AI & DS), for organizing and managing the event seamlessly.

We also thank all the student coordinators and volunteers for their dedication and valuable support throughout the event. A special acknowledgment to all the faculty members of the AI & DS department for their continuous assistance in conducting Hackwell’26 successfully.

We extend our sincere thanks to all the participants of Hackwell’26, whose enthusiasm, innovation, and competitive spirit made this event truly vibrant and impactful.

Finally, we thank all those who contributed directly and indirectly in making Hackwell’26 a remarkable success.

Hackwell’26 was not just an event, but a celebration of innovation, teamwork, and learning.

See you all in Hackwell’27!
